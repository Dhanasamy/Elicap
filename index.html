HOW TO USE ELICAP


Below are some tips and guides for each feature, explainations
of each category and how to use them to your advantage.
Never miss opportunities.

KEY FEATURES


The features can be found in the top right corner when you
are in any conversation.


OFF THE RECORD(OTR)

This chat provides peer to peer
communication tools that are
entirely private and all data is
wiped after OTR is turned off.
This is used to discuss anything
which you feel is particularly
private or sesitive.



PIN

When a message is pinned it
places it in the conversation for
everyone to see. This is useful
when people are not in the
current conversation and can
refer to it promptly when they
are available.



NOTIFICATIONS

Use this to manage your
notification settings on
desktop, mobile and email.
If 'Unread Alerts' is turned
on, you'll receive an email
notifying you of your
unread messages.


SECURE VIDEO
CALLING

Click the camera to initiate a
request. This can be used in direct
conversation and private groups.
Videos are automatically encrypted
end to end so your conversation is
completely private



MENTION

When you are mentioned,
you'll receive a notification
email to be sure you never
miss our on important
information.
(this can be changed in
notification settings).



FILES

Click this icon to find files
that have been sent and
received in your current
selected conversation.


STAR

Starring a message places it in
the 'starred messages' section.
This is useful as a reminder or
feel thats it is particularly
important. This is private, only
you are able to see this.




TOPIC

Use this to view or change
the topic of your current
conversation. This can be
within the channel, direct
messages and group
conversations.




USER INFO

Click here to find
information on users in
your current
conversation. From here
you are able to start new
direct conversations or
enter current ones.

PROFILE AND CHANNEL


These options are available on the left hand pannel. Every step
explains each ELICAP category. You can hide or leave your
conversations at any point.

PROFILE

What it is

A brief description of yourself with your contact
information and profile picture.


What it does

Show others who you are and gives them the aility
to contact you. You can use this to edit your
personal information ad check in on your status.

CHANNEL

What it is

A category section used to alert the ELICAP
community for off-market opportunities.


What it does

Used for investments and purchases or lifestyle
benefits in a private off-market community. If
interested, reply to the thread and you'll be
contacted by one of the team. If you have something
to offer, contact your banker who will arrange for it
to be posted.

DIRECT & GROUP MESSAGING


These can also be found in the left hand panel. There are two more
types of messaging platform on ELICAp explained below.

DIRECT MESSAGES

What it is

Messages that can be sent and received between you
and an individual.


What it does

Ability to communicate directly with your contacts
via text, voice or video and share documents or links
fast and easy. Here is where you can use OTR to
have a completely private conversation.

PRIVATE GROUP MESSAGING

What it is

A private group between you and all key team
members who look after your account.


What it does

The private group allows you to discuss an idea or
opportunity as quickly as possible. Send a message
to the team and they will reply as soon as they can.
OTR is also available here.
